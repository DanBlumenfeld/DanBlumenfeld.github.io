If you have a UniFi network, you may be surprised to find that Tor cannot connect, leading to a distinct lack of utility in your Tor Browser.

To unblock it from the UniFi management console, you'll need to check two places.

  1. First, ensure that the Settings->Security->Intrusion Prevention->Dark Web Blocker option is unchecked.

    Dan Blumenfeld, CC BY 4.0,by the author

  2. Second, ensure that the Tor option is unchecked in Settings->Security->Intrusion Prevention->Detection Sensitivity->Customize.
