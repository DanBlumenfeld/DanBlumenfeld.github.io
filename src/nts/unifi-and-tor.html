If you have a UniFi network, you may be surprised to find that Tor cannot connect.

To unblock it from the UniFi management console, you'll need to check two places.

First, ensure that the Settings->Security->Intrusion Prevention->Dark Web Blocker option is unchecked.

Dan Blumenfeld, CC BY 4.0,by the author

Second, ensure that the Tor option is unchecked in Settings->Security->Intrusion Prevention->Detection Sensitivity->Customize.
